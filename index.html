Hello!


I have a BSc in Computer Science and a MSc in Computational Engineering and Intelligent Systems, both awarded by the University of the Basque Country in 2016 and 2017, respectively. I am a researcher in Tekniker technological centre since 2016, where I am doing the Ph.D. in robust navigation of autonomous robots using vision techniques. I have participated in several industrial and research projects, both national and international, and I am currently involved in projects that deal with collaborative robotics, autonomous navigation of mobile robots and computer vision. As a part of the thesis, I have collaborated with the Autonomous Intelligent Systems group of the Albert Ludwig University of Freiburg, Germany, as a guest researcher in 3D active mapping and exploration.

Skills

I have spent more than 5 years in a multidisciplinary research centre programming for very different objectives, mainly in C++ and Python. Thus, I consider myself an experienced computer research scientist with a strong background in mobile robotics, artificial intelligence and autonomous systems. I also have a great interest in exoskeletons and bionic prostheses, although I have not yet taken it up professionally.

Generally speaking, I am a responsible and proactive person with excellent team working skills, initiative, and a passion for tackling challenging projects.
